TEREE

The Art of Thirteen Court Piece

A premium, high-stakes multiplayer card game of partnership strategy, bidding, and a tense Tug-of-War match collection.

Game Mechanics & Rules

👥

2v2 Partnerships

Play in partnerships. Partners sit opposite each other. Formulate tricks and collaborate closely to dominate the table.

⚔️

The Double Sir Heap

Won tricks remain on the table in a central Heap. A team only captures the entire Heap if the same player wins two consecutive tricks!

⚖️

Tug-of-War Score

Score moves dynamically along a 52-point scale. Meeting your bid pulls points to your team; failing pushes points to the opponents.

📣

Bidding & Trump

Bid the number of tricks (7 to 12) you can win. The highest bidder selects the Trump suit (Rung) based on their first 5 cards.

⚡

13-Sar Grand Slam

A high-risk 13-trick bid. The partner's hand is exposed, and the bidder controls both. Opponents winning even 1 trick results in instant loss!
